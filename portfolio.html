Featured Work

Global Resource Model (GRM)

Capacity Management & Workforce Optimization

Data Integration Dashboards

Challenge

A major retail organization lacked visibility into recruiting team capacity and utilization. With 200+ recruiters managing thousands of requisitions across multiple business functions, recruitment team leaders had no data-driven way to make daily staffing decisions when new roles came in to hire.

Approach

  • Data Integration: Consolidated recruiter-requisition mappings from Workday, requisition systems, and manual tracking into a unified data model
  • Capacity Framework: Developed weighted capacity formula accounting for job level complexity, time-to-fill metrics, and business function alignment
  • Training & Governance: Trained 200+ recruiters on consistent data entry practices
  • Dashboard: Built Power BI dashboard with role-based access showing real-time utilization

Impact

  • Daily Decision-Making: Recruitment team leaders instantly see team utilization and decide: assign to this recruiter, borrow from under-utilized teams, or escalate to senior leadership
  • Resource Allocation: Dynamic resource-sharing across teams and business functions
  • Performance Visibility: Monitor utilization, offer trends, and critically aged positions
  • Scalability: System accommodated expansion from 3 to 6+ recruiting teams

Tools:

Workday SQL Power BI

Executive Reporting & Performance Analytics

Executive Dashboard Trend Analysis

Impact

  • Time Savings: Eliminated manual PowerPoint preparation; leadership accesses live dashboard during QBR meetings

Centralized Hiring Model (CHM)

Accelerated Hiring Pipeline & Visibility Dashboard

Fast Turnaround Pipeline Tracking

Challenge

Global Tech launched a new accelerated hiring initiative to bring in high-quality candidates quickly. As a trial program with new processes, there was no centralized system to track candidate progress, pipeline stages, or hiring velocity. Recruiters were manually entering data with no visibility into the hiring funnel, making it impossible for leaders to monitor the initiative's performance or bottlenecks.

Approach

  • Quick Data Structure: Identified key data columns needed for metrics from existing manual tracking sheets
  • Integration with Assessments: Connected recruiter-entered data to assessment application systems for complete candidate journey visibility
  • Pipeline Tracking: Built metrics across hiring stages—debrief, sell calls, interviews, offers, and acceptance
  • Velocity Analytics: Calculated time-in-step metrics to identify bottlenecks (pre-screen to assessment, interview to offer, etc.)
  • Weekly Trends: Implemented week-over-week trend tracking for debrief, interview loops, sell calls, and offers
  • Leader Dashboard: Created single visibility pane for initiative stakeholders to monitor quality and quantity in real-time

Impact

  • Rapid Deployment: Built and deployed dashboard in days using manual tracking data structure—quick turnaround enabled fast decision-making
  • Pipeline Visibility: Leaders gained real-time insight into hiring funnel, candidate flow, and bottlenecks at each stage
  • Velocity Insights: Identified time-in-step delays (e.g., 39 days interview to offer) enabling process optimization
  • Quality & Quantity Balance: Metrics showed both volume (123 offers, 89 acceptances) and quality (72% offer accept rate)
  • Initiative Success: Accelerated program tracked transparently, proving concept viability and supporting expansion decisions

Tools:

Assessment Systems SQL Tableau

Early Career Dashboard (ECR)

Campus Recruitment & Intern Conversion Analytics

Data Integration Legal Compliance

Challenge

Walmart's Early Careers team needed to track intern hiring, intern-to-fulltime conversions, and campus recruitment on a different fiscal year cycle than the main recruiting organization. While candidate data existed in Workday, key fields required for comprehensive early career metrics were not captured in the main data structure. Additionally, diversity and inclusion metrics required legal and compliance approvals before they could be tracked and reported.

Approach

  • Workday Integration Partnership: Collaborated with Workday integration team to extract and expose previously unavailable fields needed for early career cohort tracking
  • FY Cycle Adaptation: Built logic to handle campus recruiting's different fiscal year calendar, separate from main recruiting operations
  • Diversity & Inclusion Metrics: Led cross-functional effort with legal and compliance teams to obtain approvals for tracking sensitive D&I data (degree type, demographics, location)
  • Comprehensive Funnel: Designed pipeline metrics tracking: Demand → Available Positions → Offers Extended → Offers Accepted across Intern and FullTime segments
  • Conversion Analytics: Built intern-to-FT conversion rate tracking with granular view across internship vs. FT segments
  • Multi-Dimensional Reporting: Built filtering by job profile, school, business area, functional alignment, and business segment

Impact

  • Strategic Tracking: Early Careers team tracks hiring progress in real-time across multiple cohorts and fiscal cycles with comprehensive demand visibility
  • Conversion Visibility: Dashboard provides intern-to-FT conversion metrics and offer acceptance rates, enabling data-driven campus hiring strategy optimization
  • Diversity Compliance: Legal-approved D&I tracking demonstrates commitment to equitable hiring with transparent reporting on candidate demographics and educational backgrounds
  • Cross-Functional Data Access: Workday integration enables self-service reporting without manual extracts or data coordination delays
  • Active Usage: Early Careers Team actively uses dashboard for ongoing reporting, hiring projections, and future offer tracking across fiscal cycles

Tools:

Workday SQL Tableau

Interactive Dashboard Wireframe

Collaborative Design & Agile Stakeholder Alignment

AI-Powered Collaborative Design

Challenge

Analytics team builds scalable dashboards for diverse stakeholders with varying expectations around design, functionality, and user experience. With a distributed team model collaborating heavily with international teams, there were significant challenges: misaligned expectations on look-and-feel, confusion over requirements, and inefficient communication across time zones and locations, resulting in rework and extended delivery timelines.

Approach

  • Interactive Wireframe Solution: Developed interactive dashboard wireframes that bridge stakeholder vision and development team understanding
  • Collaborative Design Tool: Created visual prototypes enabling stakeholders to see dashboard layout, metrics placement, and user flow before development
  • International Team Enablement: Wireframes reduce confusion and minimize late-night/early-morning calls by providing clear, visual requirements documentation
  • VS Code Integration: Built wireframes using VS Code-based development for accessibility and ease of sharing
  • AI-Powered Automation (Phase 2): Currently developing generic prompt/web-based solution enabling BI leads and analytics teams to auto-generate interactive wireframes without manual design effort

Impact

  • Requirements Clarity: Interactive wireframes eliminate ambiguity, ensuring stakeholder expectations are aligned before development begins
  • Reduced Rework: Visual prototyping reduces miscommunication-driven rework and revision cycles
  • Efficient Collaboration: Asynchronous visual communication reduces need for synchronous calls across time zones, improving team efficiency
  • Faster Time-to-Delivery: Clear requirements upfront accelerates development and stakeholder approval cycles
  • Scalable Approach: Auto-generation capability (Phase 2) will enable entire BI team to create professional wireframes independently, democratizing the design process

VS Code AI Prompting Web Technologies
